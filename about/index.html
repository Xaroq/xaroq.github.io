About

Built openly, with AI as part of the browser itself.

Xaroq is an open-source browser project focused on simplicity, transparency, and user control.

What the project is trying to do

Xaroq explores how AI can improve everyday browsing, from page understanding to navigation and assistance, without turning the browser into a black box.

The goal is to keep the product understandable, modular, and straightforward to inspect.

Principles

  • AI as a first-class feature, not an add-on
  • Transparency in how things work
  • Modular and open design
  • User control and privacy by default

Community

Xaroq is developed iteratively in public. Feedback, ideas, and contributions are part of the process.
